HISTORY

ICCCMIT 2012

The First International Conference on Communication, Computing and Information Technology - ICCCMIT 2012, was held in M.O.P.Vaishnav College for Women (Autonomous), Chennai, India on 21st and 22nd December 2012. 550 delegates representing 55 colleges from different countries attended the conference. The Department of Computer Science in association with NASSCOM, IEEE and IJCA organized the conference.

The conference was inaugurated by Mr. Atul Anand, IAS, Managing Director, ELCOT. Mr. N. R. Panicker, Founder, Executive Chairman, Chief Executive Officer and Managing Director, Accel Frontline Ltd., presided over the function. Out of 205 papers received, 88 papers wereselected and 63 papers were presented during the conference.

The selected papers were published in the International Journal of Computer Applications (IJCA). Eminent and erudite International speakers from academia and industry were part of the conference. The Valedictory Address was given by Dr. V. Jayaraman, Satish Dawan Professor & Senior Advisor(Space Applications), ISRO, Bangalore, Formerly Director, National Remote Sensing Centre, Hyderabad. Mr. H. R. Mohan, Associate Vice President - The Hindu, Presided the valedictory function. The Conference had an overwhelming response from all over the country and the world.

ICCCMIT 2014

Celebrating the 10 years of Autonomy of M.O.P. Vaishnav College, the department of Computer Science in association with Scopus Indexed International Journals and the Media Partner 'The New Indian Express', organised the Second International conference on Communication, Computing and Information Technology (ICCCMIT 2014). ICCCMIT 2014 is an exciting, prestigious event which attracts academia from Universities and Colleges throughout the world. Nearly 410 delegates attended the conference.

The conference was inaugurated by by Mr. Lakshmi Narayanan, Vice Chairman, Cognizant Technology Solutions. Eminent International speakers from academia and industry are part of the conference. The various themes of tutorials include Big Data Analysis, Mobile Apps Development, Cryptographic protocols, Cognitive modeling, IT and Business, Research paper guidelines, Augmented reality, SAP Technologies and Ethical Hacking. ICCCMIT 2014 received 128 research papers from 10 different universities and 75 different colleges. 74 accepted papers got published in the Conference Proceedings.

The selected papers 29 papers got published in the EBSCO indexed International Journal of Computer Applications (IJCA).Eleven Best extended version papers got published in SCOPUS indexed JCOMSS International Journal. The conference had Special Tracks on Mobile Apps Development challenge and Poster Presentation for students to stimulate their creative thinking.

A Panel Discussion on the theme 'Managing Big Data' was organized and moderated by Dr.Lalitha Balakrishnan, Principal, M.O.P. Vaishnav College for Women. The Panelists are Mr .K. Ramachandran (Ramskalyan), Senior Consultant, Tata Consultancy Services, Mr. Sivarajan .N, Technical Director, ChannelLive Media Inc., Mr. Vijay Kumar, Senior Engineer, C-DAC Chennai and Mr. Thiruvalluvan .M.G, Architect, Altiscale Technologies. At the Valedictory Session, Presidential Address was given by Mr.Anjan A, Director-IT, Franklin Templeton Intl Services and the Valedictory Address by Dr. Krishna Kumar S, Senior Technical Officer, DRDO. The conference was held with overwhelming response.
