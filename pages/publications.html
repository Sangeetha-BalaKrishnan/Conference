PUBLICATIONS

The accepted full papers will be published in the conference proceedings and best selected papers will be published in International Journal of Computer Applications(IJCA). Best extended version of registered papers will be considered for possible publication in the SCOPUS indexed International Journals.

Best extended version of selected paper will be considered for possible publication in the following EBSCO/SCOPUS International Journals.

  1. Journal of Internet Commerce – Taylor & Francis ISSN 1533-2861 – Indexed in Scopus/EBSCO/Inspec

  2. International Journal of Society Systems Science – InderScience ISSN 1756-2511 4 – Indexed in Inspec

  3. Pertanika Journal of Science and Technology ISSN: 0128-7680 – Indexed in SCOPUS (Elsevier), Thomson (ISI) Web of Knowledge [BIOSIS & CAB Abstracts], EBSCO & EBSCOhost
