DEPARTMENT

The Department of Computer Science began its journey with the course, B.Sc, Computer Science in the year 1994. In 1999, Bachelors in Computer Application (B.C.A) course was started. The Department further offers the Post Graduate course, M.Sc. Information Technology since 2003. Currently, the department continues to excel with 14 staff members and student strength of over 350. The USP of the department has been 'Formulation and application of computational approaches to sculpt next generation computer professionals.

The following facilitates to achieve the USP

  • Focus on application through practical oriented approach
  • Usage of podcasts as a tool for teaching
  • Emphasis on freeware tools and its usage
  • Curriculum in line with industry that helps students to be industry ready.

The department also offers industry enriched courses as Skill programmes. The Department recognizes the meritorious students and rewards them through various endowments and also offers scholarships. Department brings out the best of technical and leadership skills of the students through the department club activitie - ONLINE and the departments' thematic journal - Computer Almanac. Students of Computer Science have brought laurels by winning over 25 overall trophies from other colleges and by showing 98% placement record in Cognizant, Google, DELL, Wipro, Goldman Sachs, TCS etc.
